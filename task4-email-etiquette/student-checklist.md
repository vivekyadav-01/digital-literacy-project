# Student Checklist

## Do's

- [ ] Keep your profiles professional and update them regularly.
- [ ] Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts and personal information.
- [ ] Share academic achievements, projects, and certifications to build your professional brand.
- [ ] Verify information before sharing. Check credible sources to avoid spreading misinformation.
- [ ] Log out of shared or public computers and use strong, unique passwords for each platform.

## Don'ts

- [ ] Do not share personal details like your phone number, home address, or Aadhaar number publicly.
- [ ] Do not post or share content in anger or frustration. Pause and reflect before hitting send.
- [ ] Do not engage in or stay silent about cyberbullying. Report it using platform tools and to `cybercrime.gov.in`.
- [ ] Do not click on unknown links or download attachments from unverified senders or messages.
- [ ] Do not share OTPs, UPI PINs, or banking credentials with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank.
