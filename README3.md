## Project Overview

This project is a comprehensive exploration of **Digital Literacy** across five key areas — from professional online presence and communication to cybersecurity awareness. It is designed to build foundational digital skills necessary for navigating the modern digital landscape effectively and responsibly.

The project covers practical tasks including creating presentations, building professional portfolios, engaging with digital platforms, practicing email etiquette, and understanding cybercrime and prevention strategies.

##  Task Breakdown

###  Task 1 — Presentation
>  `task-1-presentation/`

- **Deliverables:** Canva / Prezi link or exported presentation image
- **Description:** A visually engaging presentation covering the core concepts of digital literacy, including internet safety, online communication, and responsible digital citizenship.

---

###  Task 2 — Portfolio
>  `task-2-portfolio/`

- **Deliverables:** Screenshots of professional profiles set up (e.g., LinkedIn, GitHub, or personal website)
- **Reflection Notes:** Personal reflections on the process of building a digital portfolio and lessons learned

---

###  Task 3 — Platforms
>  `task-3-platforms/`

- **Deliverables:** Screenshots from various digital platforms
- **Activity Proof:** Evidence of engagement and activity on platforms
- **Notes:** Observations and learnings from using different digital platforms for communication and collaboration

---

###  Task 4 — Email Etiquette
> `task-4-email-etiquette/`

- **Deliverables:** Sample professional email drafts for various scenarios
- **Checklist:** A comprehensive email etiquette checklist covering tone, formatting, subject lines, and more

---

###  Task 5 — Cybercrime Awareness
>  `task-5-cybercrime/`

- **Deliverables:** In-depth case study writeup on a real or simulated cybercrime incident
- **Prevention Checklist:** Actionable steps and best practices to prevent cybercrime and stay safe online
